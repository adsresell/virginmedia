Useful info & FAQs

How much cashback is available?

The reward amount depends on current Virgin Media promotions and the type of package you choose. The latest value is always shown on the official referral page once you click through.

When will I be paid?

After your service is installed or delivered and the cooling‑off period has passed, your order is checked. When Virgin Media confirms it, your reward becomes payable in Aklamio and you can withdraw it.

Who can use the scheme?

The refer‑a‑friend offer is usually for new Virgin Media customers at addresses that don’t already have a Virgin Media service. Some tariffs and staff or partner offers don’t qualify. Always read the current terms on the official site.

What if my reward doesn’t appear?

Log in to your Aklamio account to check the status. If something looks wrong, you can raise a claim via the help section on the referral portal so the team can look into it.

Ready to get started?

Use the tracked link so your order is correctly associated with the refer‑a‑friend offer, then follow the instructions on the Virgin Media and Aklamio pages.
