What Makes This Referral Offer Different?

You still get Virgin Media’s usual offers and bundles, but by using the referral journey you also unlock an additional cash reward when the terms are met. There’s no extra cost for using the scheme, and everything is managed securely via Aklamio.

FAQs

How much cashback can we get?

The reward value can change with promotions and the type of package you choose. The current amount is always shown on the Virgin Media referral page when you click through.

When will my reward be paid?

After your service is installed and the cooling-off period has passed, your order is checked. Once Virgin Media confirms it, your reward will move to confirmed in Aklamio and you’ll be able to request payment.

Who is the refer-a-friend scheme for?

It’s normally available to new Virgin Media customers at eligible addresses. Existing customers, staff tariffs and some other special offers usually don’t qualify. Always read the latest terms on the official pages.

What if my cashback doesn’t show?

Log in to your Aklamio account to check the tracking status of your referral. If something looks wrong, you can raise a claim using the support options on the referral portal so the team can review your order.
